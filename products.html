What is a Smartphone?

Posted on 11/19/2012

A Smartphone is a cell phone with computer like abilities. Smartphones allow business people to connect with clients through e-mail, text, instant messaging, or even social media. With a mobile operating system like iOS6 or Android, smartphones are much more powerful than a regular cell phone. They may have touchscreen capabilities or use a different way to navigate the GUI like a scroll ball. Most smartphones also enable you to use GPS capabilities and include a highdefinition camera.
